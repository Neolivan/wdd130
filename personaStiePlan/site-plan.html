Overview

Purpose

How many times have you had surprises when you went to a supermarket? Sometimes your calculations were different than the supermarket cashiers? My proposal is to make a website where you can create your buy list, quoting the product and quantity and the price and you will know exactly how much it will cost, it will simplify your budgets, without any surprises in the future.

Audience

This web page will be for everyone that wants to have total control of your boughts.

Content

Home page

Overview Planning is all that means in our lives, to feel in the control of the situation the people need to plan it before, what is the best situation to be in the control as the time that we do our bought? No one likes surprises at the final of your buy mainly in the total amount, the purpose of this site is to help you to plan and calculate how much you will expand with it. This application is simple to use and her design is super user-friendly, in a few steps you can plan yourself to not have any surprises at the final of your bought.

MyList Application

How to use it? In simple steps you can add the item to your cart it will open a popup that will ask you the name of the product, the unit price, and the quantity, you will add the item and on the screen, you can see all of your items and the total amount, and it probably will be similar to the what you will pay on the supermarket or other stuff like that.
